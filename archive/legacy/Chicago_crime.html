Exploring Chicago Crime Data

Lesson Goals¶

In this tutorial, we will analyze crimes data from Data Government link. The dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in Baltimore from 10/30/1963-7/18/2020.
